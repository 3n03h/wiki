## Materials
* Makey Makey
* Raspberry Pi
* KHD Robot kit
* Monitors
* Crafts material (TGR) + card board + BBQ sticks + duct tape + metal wire
* Straws
* Ask kids for broken toys
* Toothbrushes (IKEA)
* Tools (Scissors, screwdrivers)

1 x 4 -12 Megaohm resistor, 
1 x 200ohm resistor for LED (or something similar)

1 x LDR 
1 x miniservo

1 x buzzer
